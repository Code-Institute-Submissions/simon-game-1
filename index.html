How to play?

To start the game press the green button in the centre of the gameboard

Once you have started the game the start button becomes reset

You can reset your current progress at any moment by clicking on the reset button

  • As soon as you start/reset Simon will play the first sound that correspond to one of the colours
  • You should repeat the sound by pressing the coloured button
  • If you repeat the sequence correctly Simon will play the sequence with an additional sound
  • You should repeat the new sequence
  • The game continues until you make a mistake or WIN by reaching level 20 (current level is displayed in the centre next to the green button)
